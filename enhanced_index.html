Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Coin Trade.

How do I deposit funds into my account?
You can deposit funds through bank transfer, credit/debit card, or by transferring cryptocurrencies from another wallet. Simply go to your wallet section and select "Deposit" to see all available options.
Is Coin Trade secure and regulated?
Yes, Coin Trade is fully regulated and compliant with Canadian financial regulations. We use bank-level security, including cold storage for funds, two-factor authentication, and advanced encryption to protect your assets.
How do I start trading cryptocurrencies?
After creating your account and completing verification, deposit funds into your wallet. Then navigate to the trading section, select your desired trading pair, and place your buy or sell orders using our intuitive trading interface.
What are the trading fees?
Our trading fees are competitive and transparent. Maker fees start at 0.1% and taker fees at 0.15%. Higher trading volumes qualify for reduced fees. Check our pricing page for the complete fee structure.
Can I use Coin Trade on my mobile device?
Absolutely! We offer full-featured mobile apps for both iOS and Android devices. You can trade, monitor markets, and manage your portfolio on the go with the same functionality as our web platform.
